Numeric Lille

Nowadays, the numeric's world is far different from the one of a few years ago. Indeed, technologies constantly evolve which allow us to create again and again without get bored.

To work effectively, a group of enterprises can join themself together to create a numeric pole specializedinto a precise subject (as example video games or sciences). This numeric pole will concentrate his searches on the choosen subject to devellop new programs and technologies linked with.

Numeric Ecosystem

A numeric ecosystem is a group of numeric poles reunated in a location (a city as example). The main numeric pole in Lille's numeric ecosystem is Euratechnologies. Moreover, Lille is the more develloped city in the numeric's field in France.

Here wee see the different digital incubator of Lille (except euratechnologies who have her own section). There are five we will see in more detail

BlancheMaille

Located at Roubaix BlancheMaille by Euratechnologies is specialized in the digital commerce since 2016. Its goal is to support the creation and the development in the digital commerce. It provides a digital ecosystem, partners and professionals to help its customers in their approach.its goal is to enable companies to adapt to the business of tomorrow and to emerge future big names in numeric commerce. it work with many companies like amazon, capitain commerce, la redoute and have for partner la region Haut-de-France.

Plaine Image

La Plaine Image is located in Tourcoing. It is located in Tourcoing. Her specialty is everything related to audiovisual, vitrual reality and everything related to video games.It work with many editor studio like Ankama who made the famous mmorpg Dofus.

AgTech

AgTech is located at Willems near to the Belgium's border. It is specialized in all aspects of agriculture in the field of digital and new technologies. It works with start up like Agricolis and Senscrop.

Eurasanté

Located in Loos. It is specialized in the new technology int the field of the health. It works with laboratry and hospital for the sheach in the fields of the health

Pole ubiquitaire

Located in the Euratechnologies district but it is an independant pole. It work in the fields of the cyber-safety and in the robotic.
