All about Euratechnologies

The history of Euratechnologies

The building Le Blan- Lafont, heart of the pole EuraTechnologies is the result of the exemplary rehabilitation of two former textile factories built respectively in 1896 and 1900....

EuraTech
In 1989, following the general crisis of the Cotton Industry and after having been one of the 7 largest cotton mills in France with nearly 2,500 employees, the plant filed for bankruptcy. In 1996, the Urban Community of Lille opted for a conversion project: EuraTechnologies, pole of economic excellence inaugurated on March 26, 2009.
Presentation

EuraTechnologies is a 150,000 m2 business park located in Lille, with the aim of bringing together actors, projects and innovations from the ICT sector...

Story of EuraTech
It comprises a set of buildings housing research laboratories, higher education institutions and companies in the field of ICT. Spaces are made available to these organizations as well as external actors, in particular for the organization of events, conferences, workshops, forums and fairs related to innovations and new technologies. EuraTechnologies, one of whose missions is to support project promoters linked to new technologies. Thus, a start-up incubator is created, allowing entrepreneurs to mature and launch their projects and then move into an acceleration phase to reach maturity. Open since 2009, it welcomes in 2017 300 companies4, representing 4,000 jobs.


Company and implantation

EuraTechnologies has indeed managed to establish itself as the spearhead of the French digital economy through the French Tech: with 150 companies, 3,500 jobs, 100 projects in incubation each year...

Enterprises
500 annual events and a dense network of international scale (San Francisco, Dubai, Shanghai, New York, Belo Horizonte). With its expertise and aware of the challenge of spreading on the territory, EuraTechnologies diversifies its offer with Blanchemaille by EuraTechnologies, a new place dedicated to hosting and developing champions in e-commerce with more than 20 entrepreneurs and startups, 30 partners and a research laboratory specializing in e-commerce. There are 145 companies operating in a digital ecosystem conducive to entrepreneurship and "Business Development". At the same time incubator and accelerator of startups, EuraTechnologies develops 100 hectares of digital opportunities and aims to bring out and support future digital champions. Entrepreneurs, startups, SMEs, Corporate, large groups, pure-players ... who wish to develop innovative projects at the heart of this unique ecosystem of investors (VC's and Business Angels), mentors, researchers (public and private), schools, training centers research, demonstrators.

Poles' Presentation

Here wee see the different digital incubator of Lille (except euratechnologies who have her own section). There are five we will see in more detail

BlancheMaille

BlancheMaille is located at Roubaix and it is a branch of Euratechnologies, it is specialized in the digital commerce since 2016. Its goal is to support the creation and the development in the digital commerce. It provides a digital ecosystem, partners and professionals to help its customers in their approach. Its goal is to enable companies to adapt to the business of tomorrow and to emerge future big names in numeric commerce. It work with many companies like amazon, capitain commerce, la redoute and have for partner la region Haut-de-France.

Plaine Image

La Plaine Image is located in Tourcoing. Her specialty is everything related to audiovisual, vitrual reality and everything related to video games.It work with many editor studio like Ankama who made the famous mmorpg Dofus.

AgTech

AgTech is located at Willems near to the Belgium's border. It is specialized in all aspects of agriculture in the field of digital and new technologies. It works with start up like Agricolis and Senscrop.

Eurasanté

Eurasanté is located in Loos. It is specialized in the new technology in the field of the health. It works with laboratry and hospital for the sheach in the fields of the health

Pole ubiquitaire

Pole ubiquitaire is located in the Euratechnologies district but it is an independant pole. It work in the fields of the cyber-safety and in the robotic.
