Numeric Lille

Nowadays, the numeric's world is far different from the one of a few years ago. Indeed, technologies constantly evolve which allow us to create again and again without get bored.

To work effectively, a group of enterprises can join themself together to create a numeric pole specializedinto a precise subject (as example video games or sciences). This numeric pole will concentrate his searches on the choosen subject to devellop new programs and technologies linked with.

A numeric ecosystem is a group of numeric poles reunated in a location (a city as example). The main numeric pole in Lille's numeric ecosystem is Euratechnologies. Moreover, Lille is the more develloped city in the numeric's field in France.

All about EuraTechnologies

The history of EuraTechnologies

The building Le Blan- Lafont, heart of the pole EuraTechnologies is the result of the exemplary rehabilitation of two former textile factories built respectively in 1896 and 1900....

The history of EuraTechnologies
In 1989, following the general crisis of the Cotton Industry and after having been one of the 7 largest cotton mills in France with nearly 2,500 employees, the plant filed for bankruptcy. In 1996, the Urban Community of Lille opted for a conversion project: EuraTechnologies, pole of economic excellence inaugurated on March 26, 2009.
Euratechnologies: 300 compagnies and 4000 jobs!

EuraTechnologies is a 150,000 m2 business park located in Lille, with the aim of bringing together actors, projects and innovations from the ICT sector...

Presentation
It comprises a set of buildings housing research laboratories, higher education institutions and companies in the field of ICT. Spaces are made available to these organizations as well as external actors, in particular for the organization of events, conferences, workshops, forums and fairs related to innovations and new technologies. EuraTechnologies, one of whose missions is to support project promoters linked to new technologies. Thus, a start-up incubator is created, allowing entrepreneurs to mature and launch their projects and then move into an acceleration phase to reach maturity. Open since 2009, it welcomes in 2017 300 companies, representing 4,000 jobs.

500 annual events and a dense network of international scale (San Francisco, Dubai, Shanghai, New York, Belo Horizonte). With its expertise and aware of the challenge of spreading on the territory, EuraTechnologies diversifies its offer with Blanchemaille by EuraTechnologies, a new place dedicated to hosting and developing champions in e-commerce with more than 20 entrepreneurs and startups, 30 partners and a research laboratory specializing in e-commerce.

There are 145 companies operating in a digital ecosystem conducive to entrepreneurship and "Business Development". At the same time incubator and accelerator of startups, EuraTechnologies develops 100 hectares of digital opportunities and aims to bring out and support future digital champions.

Entrepreneurs, startups, SMEs, Corporate, large groups, pure-players ... who wish to develop innovative projects at the heart of this unique ecosystem of investors (VC's and Business Angels),mentors, researchers (public and private), schools, training centers research, demonstrators.
